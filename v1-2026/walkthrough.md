## Walkthrough

Stuck? Here is how to beat the game. Spoilers ahead, obviously.

**1. Collect the easy items**

From the Dock, pick up the **Map on Barrel** (it becomes the "AI-Enhanced Map" in your inventory). Then walk to the Tavern via the stone archway and pick up the **Mug of Grog** sitting on the bar counter.

**2. Get the Rubber Chicken**

Walk back to the Dock and take the jungle path on the left. In the Jungle, pick up the item from the **Rubber Tree** to get the "Rubber Chicken with a Pulley in the Middle." Classic.

**3. Trade the chicken for a key**

Return to the Tavern. You can either Give the Rubber Chicken directly to ChatGPeeTee the Bartender, or Talk to him and select the **[Give Rubber Chicken]** dialogue option. His AI brain short-circuits, reboots, and hands you a **Rusty Key**.

**4. Open the treasure chest**

Walk to the Beach (the sandy path on the right side of the Dock). Open the **Treasure Chest** while the Rusty Key is in your inventory. Inside you will find a **Golden USB Drive** labelled "GPT-6 Weights."

**5. Find the Voodoo Lady's cave**

Go back to the Dock, take the jungle path, and in the Jungle, walk into the glowing cave entrance on the right. Inside, talk to **The Voodoo Lady** and ask her what items she needs. She will tell you to place three things in the cauldron: the USB of forbidden knowledge, the map of hallucinations, and the grog that dissolves reality.

**6. Brew the potion**

Select each of the three items from your inventory and Use them on the **Cauldron**:

- Golden USB Drive
- AI-Enhanced Map
- Mug of Grog

Order does not matter. Once all three are in, the potion activates, the game is won, and the credits roll.

**Optional fun:** Talk to Murray the Skull on the Dock, chat with Governor Elaine Markup in the Jungle, and Look At everything for extra jokes. The Notice Board and Back Door in the Tavern have flavour text too.
